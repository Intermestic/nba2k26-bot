# NBA Award Trophy Designs

## The Michael Jordan Trophy (MVP)
- Gold trophy with figure in iconic jumping pose
- Red velvet curtain backdrop in official photos
- Features a basketball player silhouette mid-air
- Elegant gold finish on black base

## The Hakeem Olajuwon Trophy (DPOY)
- Named after defensive legend Hakeem Olajuwon
- Features defensive stance figure
- Gold finish similar to MVP trophy

## The Wilt Chamberlain Trophy (ROY)
- Named after Wilt Chamberlain for his legendary rookie season
- Features basketball player figure
- Gold trophy on base

## The John Havlicek Trophy (6MOY)
- Named after John Havlicek, known for bench excellence
- Features player figure
- Gold finish on base

## Design Notes for Generation
- All trophies are gold/bronze metallic finish
- Feature basketball player figures in action poses
- Mounted on elegant black or dark bases
- Modern, sleek design aesthetic
